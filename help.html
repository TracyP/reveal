How to Play

Reveal is a daily word puzzle. Each day, all players around the world are given the same mystery word to uncover, one letter at a time.

Rules

  • The word is between 6–9 letters long. Its length is shown to you from the start.
  • Click letters to guess if they’re in the word.
  • If you guess correctly, that letter is revealed everywhere it appears.
  • If the letter is not in the word, it’s marked in red on the keyboard.
  • Hints start after three guesses. From your 4th guess onward, a new letter will be revealed as a grey hint each time you’re wrong.
  • Each letter is only hinted once. If a hint reveals a letter that appears multiple times, all its positions will show.
  • The game ends once all letters of the word are revealed, whether by guessing or hints.

Scoring

  • Correct Letters: How many letters you revealed by guessing correctly.
  • Hints: How many letters were revealed to you as hints.
  • Time Taken: How long you took to complete the puzzle.
  • Streaks are tracked locally — play daily to keep your streak going!

Sharing

Once you finish, click Copy to share your stats with friends — the word is never revealed in the summary!
