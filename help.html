How to Play Reveal

Each day, everyone gets the same hidden word. Your goal is to guess all its letters.

Rules

  • Start with a blank word — no hints.
  • Tap letters to guess them.
  • If correct, all positions of that letter are revealed in green.
  • If incorrect, the letter turns red on the keyboard, and the game reveals a new letter (in grey) as a hint.
  • All instances of any revealed letter (guessed or hinted) are shown at once.
  • The game ends when all letters have been revealed.

Scoring

You’ll see:

  • Correct Letters: how many letters you revealed by guessing.
  • Hints: how many letters were revealed automatically after wrong guesses.
  • Time: how long you took to complete the puzzle.

Strategy Tips

  • Start with common letters like E, A, R, T, S.
  • Guess cautiously — wrong guesses lead to hints, which lower your score.
  • Watch out for repeated letters — they all appear at once!
